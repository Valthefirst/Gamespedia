Dante

Devil May Cry

In the Devil May Cry series, players play as Dante, son of the demon knight Sparda and a human called Eve. He is a nonchalant, cool and stylish guy. When he fights enemies, he always carries his guns, Ebony and Ivory, and his sword, Rebellion, given to him by his father. What makes him so cool is that he has no care in the world and when fighting enemies, he taunts and plays with them.

Ebony & Ivory
Ebony & Ivory are the main guns he uses to hunt demons. Ebony shoots bullets that pierce demons' skins and Ivory packs and a bang. He received them from Nell Goldstein, a master gunsmith, right before she died. They are her masterpiece.

Rebellion
Given to him when he was a child, by his father, Rebellion contains a part of his father's power and is able fusion together demon and human parts of a person.

Sparda
The Legendary Dark Knight Sparda is Dante's father. He protected humans from Mundus when he tried to take over the human world.

Vergil
Vergil is the older twin brother of Dante. They have had a lasting rivalry where they battle each other to death.

Nero
Nero is the only son of Vergil. He now protects the human world in the absence of Dante and Vergil.
