Kirby

星のカービィ

In the Kirby series, players control Kirby, the pink puffball from Dream Land on Planet Popstar, on adventures to stop a bad guy who’s causing trouble. Kirby’s always cheerful and enjoys eating, naps, and hanging out with his friends. He’s 8 inches tall and his weight is unknown. He can eat almost anything, no matter the size, and can copy the abilities of whatever he inhales!

Inhale
Kirby's inhale can allow him to eat almost anything! He can then shoot them back out in the form of a star, or copy their abilities.

Warp Star
Kirby can ride on his Warp Star to fly large distances very quickly. It's unclear what relation he has to it, but he has it whenever he needs it. It can travel even faster than light.

Food
He likes eating, and will stop at nothing to have a good snack, sometimes getting the best of him.
